UNO Ultra Spectrum Rulebook

UNO: Ultra Spectrum is an expanded version of UNO and Full Spectrum, incorporating new colors, dark/light counterparts, DOS mechanics, custom cards, and crossover rules. Sections are visually separated with a gradient bar.

1. Game Levels

Level 1: UNO Rules

  • Base game rules as published by Mattel.
  • Players are dealt 7 cards.

Level 2: Full Spectrum Rules

  • Expanded light colors: Orange, Purple, Teal, Blossom Pink, Hot Pink, Light Green.
  • Players are dealt 10 cards instead of 7 due to more colors.
  • Swap Pack, Reverse Pack, and other official add-ons may be included.

Level 3: Ultra Spectrum Light Rules

  • Light color placement rules:
    1. Light → Light matching.
  • Custom mistake system applies (see below).

Level 4: Ultra Spectrum Dark Rules

  • Introduces dark counterparts: Dark Red, Dark Green, Dark Yellow, Dark Blue.
  • Requires UNO Show No Mercy expansion (without coins).
  • Placement rules:
    1. Light → Light
    2. Dark → Dark
    3. Dark → Light (only if same color counterpart)
    4. Light → Dark (only if same color counterpart)

Level 5: Advanced Custom Rules

  • DOS Mechanics:
    • 2-Wild Card: Choose two colors for next turn(s).
    • Skip Decision Option: Place a “2” card on top of any color instead of choosing colors.
    • Light/Dark Counterpart Rule: When choosing colors, light or dark may be selected. If a player plays the dark version of a chosen color, that dark color becomes active.
  • Infinity Card: Player who plays it loses immediately and picks up the entire deck.
  • -2 and -4 Cards: Remove that many cards from your hand instead of drawing.
  • LOCO Wheels Rule: Slap Six. Last player to slap draws 1 card.
  • UNO Infinity Cards (optional):
    • +1 Card: Adds 1 card to a target player.
    • Targeted +2 Card: Choose a specific player to draw 2 cards.
    • Targeted Draw Four: Choose a specific player to draw 4 cards.
    • Targeted Draw One: Choose a specific player to draw 1 card.

2. All Playable Card Colors

The playable colors in Ultra Spectrum UNO:

Red Green Light Green Yellow Blue Orange Teal Purple Blossom Pink Hot Pink Dark Red Dark Green Dark Yellow Dark Blue

3. Custom Mistake System

  • First mistake: draw “half a card” (no penalty, warning only).
  • Second mistake: draw 1 card.
  • After two mistakes, lives reset.
