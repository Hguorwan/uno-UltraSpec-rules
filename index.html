UNO Ultra Spectrum Deluxe Edition

UNO Ultra Spectrum Deluxe is a fork of UNO Full Spectrum. It expands gameplay with new colors, light/dark counterparts, custom cards, crossover rules from other UNO variants, and exclusive Deluxe mechanics.

Level 1: UNO Rules

  • Base game rules as published by Mattel.
  • Players are dealt 7 cards.

Level 2: Full Spectrum Rules

  • Expanded colors: Orange, Purple, Teal, Blossom Pink, Hot Pink, Light Green.
  • Players are dealt 10 cards instead of 7 due to more colors.
  • Swap Pack, Reverse Pack, and other official add-ons may be included.

Level 3: Ultra Spectrum Light Rules

  • Light colors may only be played on other light colors of the same value or declared color.
  • Custom mistake system applies (see below).

Level 4: Ultra Spectrum Dark Rules

  • Introduces dark counterparts: Dark Red, Dark Yellow, Dark Green, Dark Blue.
  • Requires UNO Show No Mercy expansion (without coins).
  • Placement rules:
    1. Light → Light
    2. Dark → Dark
    3. Dark → Light (only if same color counterpart)
    4. Light → Dark (only if same color counterpart)

Level 5: Advanced Custom Rules

  • DOS Mechanics
    • 2-Wild Card: Choose two colors for play.
    • Skip Decision: Place a “2” card instead of declaring colors.
    • Counterparts: Dark/Light may be chosen together (e.g., Red + Dark Red).
  • Infinity Card: Player loses instantly and takes the entire deck.
  • -2 and -4 Cards: Remove that many cards from your hand.
  • LOCO Wheels Rule: Slap Six – last player to slap draws 1 card.
  • UNO Infinity Cards:
    • +1 Card
    • Targeted +2
    • Targeted Draw Four
    • Targeted Draw One

Level 6: Deluxe Rules

  • Liars UNO & House Rules UNO Variants: Red, Green, Yellow, and Blue gain Liars and House Rules versions.
    • If a Liars card is played face-down and proven false (or unrealistic), the player takes a penalty.
  • Draw 15 & Challenge (20): Draw 15 forces the next player to take 15 cards. If a Challenge card is played, it upgrades to 20.
  • No Mercy Limit: Players are eliminated if they exceed 100 cards.
  • ONO 99 Card:
    • Can be countered by a “9” of any color.
    • If used on a player with UNO, that player is eliminated by exceeding the card limit.
    • Draw 99 effect only activates when used on a UNO player.
  • The 10 Card (special, one per deck):
    • Acts as a normal 10, OR
    • Makes a player draw 10, OR
    • Skips two players, OR
    • Triggers a Wild Sequence (allowing chaining of special cards).

All Playable Card Colors (in order)

Red Orange Yellow Green Light Green Teal Blue Purple Blossom Pink Hot Pink Dark Red Dark Yellow Dark Green Dark Blue

Custom Mistake System

  • First mistake: draw “half a card” (warning only).
  • Second mistake: draw 1 card.
  • After two mistakes, lives reset.
